FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before working with Digi Vijay. Can't find your answer? Ask us directly!

How long does SEO take to show results?
+
SEO is a long-term strategy. Most businesses see initial improvements within 30–60 days, with significant ranking gains in 3–6 months. For local searches in Kanchipuram, results can come faster since competition is lower than major metros.
What is your minimum budget for Google Ads?
+
We recommend a minimum ad spend of ₹5,000–₹10,000/month for Google Ads to see meaningful results. Our management fee is separate from the ad budget. We'll help you choose the right budget based on your industry and goals.
Do you work with businesses outside Kanchipuram?
+
Absolutely! While we specialize in Kanchipuram, we serve clients across Tamil Nadu including Chennai, Chengalpattu, Vellore, Coimbatore, and beyond. Our local expertise benefits any Tamil Nadu business.
Do you create Tamil language content?
+
Yes! We create bilingual Tamil and English content across all our services — social media posts, ad copy, website content, and video scripts. Tamil content consistently outperforms English-only content for local Tamil Nadu audiences.
How do I track my campaign performance?
+
You'll receive monthly reports covering traffic, rankings, leads, ad performance, and ROI. We also give you access to a live dashboard and are available on WhatsApp whenever you have questions.
Are there any hidden fees or lock-in contracts?
+
No hidden fees, ever. Our pricing is fully transparent from day one. We offer month-to-month contracts — no minimum commitment required. We earn your trust every single month with results.
What industries do you specialize in?
+
We have experience with silk sarees & handlooms, restaurants & food, healthcare & clinics, education & coaching centres, real estate, retail shops, wedding services, and professional services. If you're a local Tamil Nadu business, we can help.
